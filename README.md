## Project Overview

This project focuses on designing an interactive **Amazon Supply Chain & Operations Analytics Dashboard** using **SQL** and **Microsoft Power BI**. The objective was to transform raw transactional data into meaningful business insights that support data-driven decision-making across various supply chain functions.

The project follows a structured analytics workflow, beginning with data understanding and progressing through data cleaning, exploratory data analysis (EDA), and interactive dashboard development.

---

# Project Workflow

## 📌 Phase 1 – Data Understanding

The project began with a detailed examination of the dataset to understand its structure, business entities, and relationships.

### Activities Performed

* Explored the complete dataset structure.
* Studied all business tables and their attributes.
* Understood relationships between customers, orders, products, sellers, warehouses, inventory, deliveries, payments, returns, and calendar tables.
* Identified primary keys and foreign keys.
* Understood business processes represented within the dataset.
* Defined analytical objectives and dashboard requirements.

**Outcome:**
A complete understanding of the dataset that established the foundation for subsequent project phases.

---

## 🧹 Phase 2 – Data Cleaning

The dataset was validated and prepared for analysis using SQL to ensure accuracy, consistency, and reliability.

### Activities Performed

* Validated primary and foreign keys.
* Checked referential integrity across related tables.
* Identified missing values and inconsistent records.
* Removed duplicate records.
* Verified appropriate data types.
* Standardized categorical values.
* Validated business rules and logical constraints.
* Performed date consistency checks.
* Verified calculated fields and business metrics.

**Outcome:**
A clean, reliable, and analysis-ready dataset suitable for reporting and visualization.

---

## 📊 Phase 3 – Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

Exploratory analysis was performed using SQL to understand business performance and identify meaningful trends before dashboard development.

### Activities Performed

* Analyzed revenue and order performance.
* Evaluated customer demographics and purchasing behaviour.
* Identified high-performing products and categories.
* Analyzed inventory availability and warehouse utilization.
* Evaluated seller performance and operational efficiency.
* Assessed delivery performance and logistics metrics.
* Examined returns, refunds, and payment trends.
* Identified key business KPIs.
* Generated insights to support dashboard planning.

**Outcome:**
Business insights and KPIs were established, providing the analytical foundation for the Power BI dashboards.

---

## 📈 Phase 4 – Dashboard Design & Development

Interactive dashboards were developed in Microsoft Power BI to visualize business performance across different operational areas.

### Activities Performed

* Imported the cleaned dataset into Power BI.
* Built the relational data model.
* Created DAX measures and calculated KPIs.
* Designed interactive dashboard layouts.
* Implemented slicers and cross-filtering.
* Added page navigation and bookmarks.
* Designed custom KPI cards.
* Developed insight pop-up cards with badges and tooltips.
* Applied a consistent Amazon-inspired visual theme.

### Dashboard Pages

* Executive Overview
* Sales Performance
* Customer Insights
* Inventory Analytics
* Seller Performance
* Delivery Analytics
* Returns & Payments Analysis

**Outcome:**
A fully interactive Business Intelligence dashboard enabling users to monitor KPIs, explore business trends, and make informed decisions.
